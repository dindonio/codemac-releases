# Privacy Policy for CodeMac++

**Last Updated:** March 22, 2026

Antonio Scognamiglio ("we", "our", "us") built CodeMac++ as a native macOS code editor. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your data.

## Data We Do NOT Collect

CodeMac++ does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, analytics, telemetry, crash reports, or usage statistics. The app runs entirely on your Mac with no connection to our servers.

## AI Features (Optional)

CodeMac++ includes optional AI-powered code assistance features that are **disabled by default**. When you choose to enable them:

- **What is sent:** Selected code snippets or file excerpts are transmitted to the AI provider you choose for processing.
- **Where it is sent:** Depending on your selection, data is sent to one of these third-party providers:
  - **Anthropic** (Claude) — [anthropic.com/privacy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy)
  - **OpenAI** (GPT) — [openai.com/privacy](https://openai.com/privacy)
  - **Google** (Gemini) — [ai.google.dev/terms](https://ai.google.dev/terms)
- **API Keys:** You provide your own API key (BYOK). Keys are stored locally in the macOS Keychain on your device and are never transmitted to us.
- **Consent:** A consent dialog is shown before AI features are activated. You can disable AI features at any time in Settings.

CodeMac++ does not log, store, or retain any data sent to or received from AI providers.

## Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

- **Preferences:** Theme, font size, editor settings — stored in UserDefaults.
- **API Keys:** Stored in the macOS Keychain (encrypted by the operating system).
- **Security-Scoped Bookmarks:** File/folder access permissions for App Sandbox compliance — stored in UserDefaults.
- **Recent Files List:** Paths of recently opened files — stored in UserDefaults.

None of this data is transmitted externally.

## Network Connections

CodeMac++ makes network connections **only** when you use AI features, connecting exclusively to the AI provider API endpoint you have configured. No other network connections are made.

## Third-Party Services

CodeMac++ does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

CodeMac++ does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
